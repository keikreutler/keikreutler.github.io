Forever Alpha

foreveralpha.club

Forever Alpha is an experiential platform for biohacking and P2P learning. Over the course of 2016, Schillerpalais project space hosted a series of Forever Alpha events focused on DIY biology.

These events have contributed toward opening the project space as a public, cooperatively-run biohacklab.

I initiated the Forever Alpha concept, and I coordinated events and co-managed the project space.
