Open Space Observatory

OPENSPACEOBSERVATORY.ORG

Open Space Observatory is an initiative for the observation of satellites, spacecraft, and space junk.

The initiative includes mapping and cataloging information on satellites, embedding civic space technology in public settings in collaboration with the Libre Space Foundation, and proposing alternative narratives of observation through speculative design.

The Open Space Observatory was included in The New Observatory group exhibition at FACT Liverpool in 2017.

I coordinate the initiative's design, development, and related workshops.
