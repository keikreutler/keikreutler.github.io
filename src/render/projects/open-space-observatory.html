Open Space Observatory

Open Space Observatory is an initiative for the observation of satellites, spacecraft, and space junk.

The OSO includes opening satellite observatory sites, the support and development of open source hardware and software working in collaboration with the Libre Space Foundation, and the design of a modular observatory.

Additionally, the initiative researches the deep history of astronomical architectures, speculative trajectories of their future use, and the potential for collective narratives of satellite constellations.

The Open Space Observatory was included in The New Observatory group exhibition at FACT Liverpool in 2017.

I coordinate the initiative's design, development, and related workshops. Collaborators include studiofolder.it, Fabi Borges and the Global Ports Authority project.
