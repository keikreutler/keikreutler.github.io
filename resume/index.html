Professional Experience

Inner Library, Consultant, 2024 to Present
I advise on organizational design, memory, and strategy, and I facilitate workshops that help organizations explore how they'll evolve over time. I also write and ghostwrite essays, reports, and narrative slide decks on culture and technology for clients.

Ethereum Foundation Summer of Protocols, Core Researcher, 2023
I researched and published work on the concept of memory in relation to “protocols,” specifically focused on how the concept of memory from antiquity to the present changed with the advent of computing.

Gnosis, Director of Strategy and Communications, 2017 to 2022
I led strategy and communications at Gnosis, building decentralized software infrastructure for the Ethereum ecosystem with a focus on decentralized governance. I executed core project announcements, including Safe (formerly Gnosis Safe), Gnosis Protocol, and GnosisDAO, which involved launching, maintaining, and growing community channels like newsletters, Discord servers, and social media with over 100K followers. In this role, I wore multiple hats, informing strategic direction for the company, managing the communications team, and writing technical product communications. I also authored influential essays such as A Prehistory of DAOs that shaped the industry.
