Professional Experience

Inner Library, Consulting, 2024 to Present
I advise on organizational design, memory, and strategy, as well as write and ghostwrite on culture and technology for clients.

Ethereum Foundation Summer of Protocols, Core Researcher, 2023
I researched and published work on the concept of memory in relation to “protocols,” specifically focused on how the concept of memory from antiquity to the present changed with the advent of computing.

Gnosis, Director of Strategy and Communications, Berlin, 2017 to 2022
I led strategy and communications at Gnosis, building decentralized software infrastructure for the Ethereum ecosystem. I managed all core product launches, including for Safe (formerly Gnosis Safe), CowSwap (formerly Gnosis Protocol), and GnosisDAO, which combined facilitate the secure exchange of more than 100 Billion in assets today. I also maintained all community channels, including newsletters, social media, and Discord servers.

Gnosis Guild, Co‑Creator, Berlin, 2020 to 2022
While working at Gnosis, I led Gnosis Guild, a small team incubated by Gnosis that established an open standard to support composable, modular software for organizations, which was featured on the MIT Computational Law Report.1 As part of Gnosis Guild, I also authored influential essays on our blog that shaped the industry.
