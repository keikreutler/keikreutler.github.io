Professional Experience

Inner Library, Consulting, 2024 to Present
I advise on organizational design, memory, and strategy, and I facilitate workshops that help organizations explore how they'll evolve over time. I also write and ghostwrite essays, reports, and narrative slide decks on culture and technology for clients.

Ethereum Foundation Summer of Protocols, Core Researcher, 2023
I researched and published work on the concept of memory in relation to “protocols,” specifically focused on how the concept of memory from antiquity to the present changed with the advent of computing.

Gnosis, Director of Strategy and Communications, 2017 to 2022
I led strategy and communications at Gnosis, building decentralized software infrastructure for the Ethereum ecosystem with a focus on decentralized governance. I shaped strategic direction, and I executed core project announcements, including Safe (formerly Gnosis Safe), Gnosis Protocol, and GnosisDAO. I launched, maintained, and grew community channels, like the newsletter, social media, and Discord servers, and I managed the communications team. I wrote technical product communications and influential essays that shaped the industry.

Gnosis Guild, Co‑Creator, 2020 to 2022
While working at Gnosis, I led Gnosis Guild, a small team incubated by Gnosis that established an open standard to support composable, modular software for organizations, which was featured on the MIT Computational Law Report.1
