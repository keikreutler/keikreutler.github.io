Professional Experience

Inner Library, Consulting, 2024 to Present
I write, ghostwrite, and edit essays, reports, and narrative slide decks on culture and technology. I also advise on governance design, institutional memory, and long-term strategy, and I facilitate workshops that help organizations explore how they’ll evolve over time. My consulting currently includes building a new cultural institution in the Bay Area, CA that will open to the public in 2027.

Memory Research Group (Summer of Protocols), Lead, 2024 to Present
I lead an interdisciplinary research group on memory, and I publish essays from this research for broader audiences.

Ethereum Foundation Summer of Protocols, Core Researcher, 2023
I published “Artificial Memory and Orienting Infinity,” an essay on how ideas of memory changed with the advent of computing, from classical mnemonic techniques to contemporary artificial intelligence.

Gnosis, Director of Strategy and Communications, 2017 to 2022
I worked closely with engineering teams to communicate their work on open-source software for decentralized governance. I managed the communications team through major product launches, built relationships with journalists, and positioned company leadership in the press and at public events. I authored essays that reached into wider cultural conversation, including “A Prehistory of DAOs,” and I founded Gnosis Guild, a small team within the company that established an open standard for modular organizational software, featured on the MIT Computational Law Report.
